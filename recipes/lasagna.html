Lasagna Recipe

How To Make Traditional Italian Lasagna:

The traditional italian lasagna is on of the most popular Dishes in the world. The key to make a true and original lasagna is to have a good quality ingeredients.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef 500g
  • Onion and Garlic 1 tbsp
  • Tomato Sauce 500ml
  • Seasoning 1 tbsp
  • Cheese 100g
  • lasagne sheets 1 pack (250g)

Method

  1. Prepare the meat base

    Mix ground beef with seasoning and add 1 egg.

  2. Cook garlic and onion

    Preheat your frying pan with 2 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil then add garlic, onion and cook until golden brown.

  3. Add tomato sauce and meat into your onion and garlic mixture

    Cook for about 10 minutes on low-medium heat

  4. Layer your lasagna sheets with your mixture

    Cover your baking tray with lasagne sheets, one layer should be about 4 sheets (Depends on the size of your baking tray).

    Then pour your tomato meat mixture on top of that One layer should be 0,5cm thick at max.

    Finally, cook for about 20 - 25 minutes on high heat (230 degree celsius)

  5. ENJOY YOUR MEAL!
